About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Vaagai Foundation (Reference: 8LOPC01) was established with the vision of creating a society where every individual has access to basic necessities, quality education, and healthcare facilities. We believe in empowering communities to become self-reliant and sustainable.

Our mission is to implement innovative solutions to address social challenges, with a focus on education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and women empowerment.

Since our inception, we have impacted thousands of lives through our various initiatives across multiple regions.
